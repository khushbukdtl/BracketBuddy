How Tournament Buddy Works

Create professional tournaments in just a few simple steps. From setup to completion, we make tournament management effortless.

See How It Works
Step 1

Enter Tournament Details

Set a name and brief description to introduce your tournament.

  • Enter a catchy tournament name
  • Add a short description and rules
  • Mention prizes and highlights
Step 2

Choose Tournament Type

Single Elimination

Fast knockout format – one loss and you're out.

Double Elimination

Get a second chance with an additional losers' bracket.

Single Round Robin

Every team plays against all others once.

Double Round Robin

Every team plays against all others twice.

Group Stages

Divide teams into pools and select top performers.

Swiss System

Intelligent pairings – best for competitive balance.

Step 3

Add Teams

Enter individual team names or simply specify the total number of teams.

  • Manually enter team names
  • Or just enter total number of teams
  • Teams will be auto-arranged in the bracket
Step 4

Download Your Bracket

  • Click "Download" to save your tournament bracket instantly.
  • The bracket will be saved as a PDF file.
  • Easily share the PDF with participants and teammates.
  • Print-ready format for offline use.
  • Keep a record of your tournament setup.
